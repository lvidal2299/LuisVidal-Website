I was born and raised in San Diego, California (though not in wealthy areas). Both my parents escaped severe poverty in Mexico and moved to the United States so that I would not have to worry about this. And even if poverty here is not as bad as in Mexico, I was still surrounded by gangs, violence, drugs, and poverty. In spite of not speaking English and not having family, both my parents went out to find a job in order to put food on the table and a roof over my head. This inspired me at a young age to decide that I, too, would make my own sacrifices for my family. My three hour commute throughout middle and high school was the foundation for the rest of my education. I can proudly say that I am the first person in my entire family and the first person in my known family history to attend university.
